Neuroevolution

  • Genetic algorithm on ANN (Artificial neural networks)
  • Algorithm :
    1. Population of ANN is generated
    2. After end of their performance, their fitness is calculated
    3. Natural selection occurs based on fitness score probabilistically
    4. Selected ones go through crossover to generate new offspring
    5. Offspring is mutated
    6. After multiple crossover, a new generation is created..we are back to step 1
